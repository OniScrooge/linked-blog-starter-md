Reciprocal of any function just means for you to reverse the fraction
1. $$sin = csc$$
2. $$cos = sec$$
3. $$tan \left( \frac{sin} {cos} \right) = cot \left( \frac{cos} {sin} \right)$$
4. $$csc = \frac{1} {sin}$$
5. $$sec = \frac{1} {cos}$$
6. $$cot \left( \frac{cos}{sin} \right) = tan \left( \frac{sin}{cos} \right)$$
